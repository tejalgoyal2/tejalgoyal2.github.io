Default Claude vs. Chibi Design System

Same content, same structure. Different design system. Hover, scroll, and click the dark/light toggle on the Chibi version.

Default Claude Design
Chibi Design System

What Default Claude Does

  • Purple gradient text on the hero heading
  • Floating gradient orb blobs in the background
  • Emoji icons in colored rounded squares on cards
  • Gradient top-border on card hover with bounce-up
  • Space Grotesk font (Claude's "safe unique" default)
  • Centered layout throughout
  • Bright purple CTA button with glow shadow

What Chibi Does Instead

  • Solid off-white text with one italic word for drama
  • Clean warm near-black background, no decoration
  • Numbered cards (01, 02, 03) with serif display titles
  • Border-weight shift on hover, no bounce or glow
  • Cormorant Garamond display + Satoshi body pairing
  • Left-aligned hero with generous whitespace
  • Inverted-contrast pill button, dark/light toggle
